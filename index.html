In Canada, over 40% of the labour force could be facing a threat from automation over the next 10-20 years.

HUMAN V. MACHINE
Historically, the advent of powerful technologies has inspired equal doses of fear and hope. But something is particularly compelling and terrifying about Artificial Intelligence, which clearly has the potential to displace labor. Rather than fighting the process, we should consider devoting resources to skills training for the many service and manufacturing workers who are displaced by machines, and preparing them for the new jobs that will emerge.
