WHAT DO WE DO?

Livelihood project is a platform for economic integration of low skilled workers whose job situation is at risk of being affected by artiﬁcial intelligence. Our mission is to help people build long-term careers that are resilient to changes in economy. We are doing this by developing a career coaching program centered around future skills training, analogous work ecosystem and behavioural-based mobile applications.
